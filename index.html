ℹ How it works
Signing — Petra signs your message via Ed25519 using the Aptos Wallet Standard (AIP-62). The wallet prefixes your message as APTOS\nmessage: ...\nnonce: ... before signing, producing a fullMessage + signature.

Verification — The Ed25519 signature is verified entirely in your browser using TweetNaCl. No data leaves your device. The fullMessage + publicKey + signature are all that's needed to cryptographically prove address ownership.
